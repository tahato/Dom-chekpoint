ABOUT

CHARLOTTE RESTAURANT & LOUNGE

Where Pacific Northwest cuisine takes a contemporary twist. Situated on the 16th floor, our restaurant & lounge offers sweeping views of Elliott Bay. Our imaginative dishes are crafted with fresh and local ingredients, paired perfectly with our carefully curated, seasonally-changing cocktails.

EXQUISITE CUISINE

CONTEMPORARY PACIFIC NORTHWEST

Charlotte Restaurant & Lounge features a contemporary approach to Pacific Northwest cuisine, showcasing elevated dishes using fresh, local ingredients served in an approachable way. Menu highlights include PNW Oysters, Stone Oven Octopus, Roasted Black Cod, and the Filet Mignon. Highlighting seasonal flavors and a classic yet contemporary style, the dessert menu offers standout options from mouthwatering Soufflé to our 'Matchamisu'.

DELECTABLE DESSERTS

ARTISTIC EDIBLE CREATIONS

Executive Pastry Chef Artis Kalsons demonstrates exceptional technique combining his passion for seasonal flavors with a contemporary approach to the classics. Chef Artis Kalsons finds inspiration from the range of flavors that are much loved by today’s guests. The dessert menu highlights include classics such as the White Chocolate Chip Soufflé, as well as seasonal specials.
